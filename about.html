Our Leadership Team

Quentin Green

DIRECTOR

Quentin is also a carpenter by trade and has worked with Clint for many years, carrying out all aspects of carpentry within the renovation building industry, from second-storey additions to internal finishing.


He has wide experience across the residential and commercial building sector and runs the office side of things for Green-Shore Builders including estimating, variations, pricing, contracts – the list goes on.




Clint Shore

DIRECTOR

Clint completed his apprenticeship as a Carpenter and Joiner before starting his own carpentry business. The business grew quickly and established itself as the preferred contractor with a number of Perth’s leading renovation builders.

As On Site Supervisor for all jobs we undertake, this hands on role by a Company Director sits really well with all our clients and is a massive point of difference to many other renovation companies. They like the fact that the person they are dealing with every day is an owner of the company.

Our Directors

Ben

SITE SUPERVISOR

Ben and our other direct employees play a very important part in the success of Green-Shore Builders. Our clients are always happy with their courtesy, care and tidiness when working in their homes. The onsite team is lead by Clint Shore (Director) and all work closely with him to ensure our clients are happy every step of the way.




Jamie

DESIGNER

Jamie has also been with us since Day 1 and works in the Designer role. He has a background in new building and his father was a builder, so he brings a unique combination and practicality and creativity to the design process. He will also design a concept that actually fits your budget, often a rarity amongst architects and designers these days. He has been running his own design company for 10 years and his flare for creating beautiful and functional layouts never fails to impress our clients.




Craig

SCHEDULER/DESIGNER

He returned as one of our carpenters and recently has taken on additional work as a scheduler, helping our Designer with working drawings/plan variations and general processes from an office perspective.
